Basics of Blockchain

Blockchain is a decentralized digital ledger that securely records transactions across multiple computers. It provides a transparent and immutable record of data, making it highly resistant to tampering and fraud.Blockchain technology forms the foundation for various applications, including cryptocurrencies like Bitcoin and Ethereum.

Applications of Blockchain Beyond Cryptocurrency

Blockchain technology has applications beyond cryptocurrency. It can revolutionize industries such as supply chain management, healthcare, finance, and more. By providing transparency, security, and decentralization, blockchain enables efficient tracking of goods, secure medical records, streamlined financial transactions, and even voting systems.
